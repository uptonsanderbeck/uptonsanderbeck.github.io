Phoebe R. Upton Sanderbeck

Director's Postdoc Fellow at Los Alamos National Lab

My name is Phoebe R. Upton Sanderbeck and I am currently a Director's Postdoc Fellow at Los Alamos National Lab. Previously, I held a postdoctoral position in the Physics & Astronomy Department at the University of California, Riverside. I earned my PhD from the University of Washington in 2018. My research interests encompass theoretical astrophysics, radiation backgrounds, the intergalactic medium, circumgalactic medium, black holes, cosmology, and more.
