Phoebe R. Upton Sanderbeck

My name is Phoebe R. Upton Sanderbeck and I am currently a Director's Postdoc Fellow at Los Alamos National Lab. I previously was a postdoc in the Physics & Astronomy Department at the University of California, Riverside. I got my PhD from the University of Washington in 2018. My research insterests are in theoretical astrophysics, radiation backgrounds, the intergalactic medium, circumgalactic medium, black holes, and cosmology.
